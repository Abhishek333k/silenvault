Mastering GST: A Business Owner's Guide for 2026

Current GST Rates in India 2026

Understanding the tax slabs is crucial for compliance. The current structure is as follows:

  • 0% (Nil): Essential goods like unbranded grains, fresh vegetables, milk, and salt.
  • 5%: Household necessities, life-saving medicines, and economy class air travel.
  • 12%: Processed food, computers, and mobile phones.
  • 18% (Standard): Most manufactured goods, capital goods, services, hair oil, and soap.
  • 28% (Luxury): Luxury cars, tobacco products, and aerated drinks.

Understanding Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)

Normally, the supplier collects tax from the buyer and pays the government. Under RCM, the liability to pay tax shifts to the recipient.

When does RCM apply?
  • Buying goods/services from an unregistered dealer.
  • Services by Goods Transport Agencies (GTA).
  • Legal services provided by advocates to a business entity.
