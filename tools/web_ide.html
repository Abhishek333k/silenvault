Frontend Development Sandbox

  • Sandboxed Execution: The output preview runs inside an isolated HTML5 iframe. It supports JavaScript execution (allow-scripts), but protects your main browser tab from malicious loops or DOM hijacking.
  • Local-First Storage: You do not need to create an account to save your code. As you type, the engine continuously saves your HTML, CSS, and JS data securely into your browser's localStorage. Refreshing the page will never delete your work.
  • Export to Production: When your prototype is finished, hit "Export .html". The IDE mathematically merges your CSS into a <style> block and your JS into a <script> block, downloading a single, production-ready index.html file to your hard drive.
