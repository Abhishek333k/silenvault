Hardware Diagnostic Guide

  • Bubble Level Test: Lay your device completely flat on a table. The bubble should center itself and turn Green. If it is permanently off-center on a flat surface, your internal gyroscope needs recalibration.
  • Accelerometer Test: The Z-Axis measures gravity. When laying flat on a desk, the Z-Axis should read approximately 9.81 m/s² (Earth's gravitational pull). Vigorously shaking the device will spike the X and Y axes.
  • Privacy & Permissions: To prevent web trackers from fingerprinting your device movements, modern browsers (like Safari on iOS) require you to manually click "Start Diagnostics" to grant temporary sensor access.
