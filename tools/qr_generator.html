What are Static QR Codes?

Static QR codes encode the data directly into the pixel matrix. Unlike "Dynamic" QR codes offered by marketing platforms, static codes do not contain a redirect link and do not require an external server to function. This makes them permanent, non-expiring, and more secure.

Data Protocol Standards

This generator supports industry-standard protocols recognized by iOS and Android:

  • Wi-Fi: Uses the WIFI:S:;T:;P:;; syntax to trigger automatic router connection.
  • vCard: Encodes contact information in the BEGIN:VCARD format for instant address book saving.
  • Action URIs: Supports tel:, sms:, and mailto: triggers.

Privacy-First Architecture

Most online QR generators intercept your data by uploading it to their servers. SilenVault utilizes an HTML5 Canvas pipeline. All mathematical computations and image rendering occur entirely within your browser's local RAM. No data is transmitted across the internet during the generation process.

Export Guidelines

For professional printing (business cards, brochures), we recommend an export size of at least 1000px and an Error Correction Level (ECL) of Medium (M) or Quartile (Q) to ensure scannability even if the physical print is slightly damaged or smudged.
