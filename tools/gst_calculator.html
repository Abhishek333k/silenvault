Enterprise Tax Calculation Directives

This terminal executes exact financial computations required for standard B2B and B2C invoicing. The algorithm automatically resolves fractional roundings to strictly adhere to governmental tax compliance protocols.

Inclusive vs. Exclusive Architecture

  • Exclusive (Adding Tax): Use this when your catalog prices do not contain tax. The engine mathematically appends the tax rate on top of your base parameter.
  • Inclusive (Extracting Tax): Use this if you receive a flat payment and must determine how much of that total belongs to the government. The engine calculates the embedded base cost.

Jurisdictional Protocols

  • Intra-State (CGST / SGST): For transactions within the same local state, the algorithm dynamically splits the tax burden 50/50 between Central and State allocations.
  • Inter-State (IGST): Check the "Inter-State" override if you are billing a client outside your registered state. The tax consolidates into a single Integrated ledger.
  • RCM Protocol: If Reverse Charge Mechanism is invoked, the algorithm zeroes the invoice tax, shifting the remittance liability entirely to the buyer.
