Diagnostic Guide

  • Dead Zone Test: Scrub your finger back and forth across the entire canvas. If the colored trail breaks or stops drawing while your finger is still down, your screen digitizer has a dead spot.
  • Multi-Touch Limit: Place as many fingers on the screen as possible. Modern devices should easily register 5 to 10 simultaneous touches.
  • Desktop Support: Don't have a touch screen? This tool fully supports standard mouse clicking and dragging to test monitors.
