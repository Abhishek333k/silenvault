What is "Borewell Flushing"?

Imagine your borewell is a glass of water, and sand has settled at the bottom. Flushing is like blowing a straw into it to clear the mess.

The Process at Sagar Borewells:

  1. Step 1: We remove your submersible pump.
  2. Step 2: We lower a heavy-duty steel air pipe to the bottom (up to 1000 ft).
  3. Step 3: Our High-Pressure Compressor blasts air at 300 PSI.
  4. Result: The pressure forces all the mud, sludge, and dirty water out of the hole. We continue until the water runs crystal clear (Sweet Water).
